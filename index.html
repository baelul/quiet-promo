about.

what is quiet?

quiet is an experience one could never forget. the idea of this event is to replicate big brother; think game show! you'll be staying in a nice japanese-style capsule hotel, complete with fancy screens for you to see our work in action. who knows who you'll meet...

where will you be located?

in the heart of lower manhattan, you will get to live with 100 or so other enthusiastic people of the city! you can experience true new york without ever having to leave your place.

amenities.

what is provided to you?

by participating, you'll get unlimited access to anything you could ever dream of.
  • free clothes
  • cereal bar
  • (normal) bar
  • endless partying!
you will also get your own cubby to sleep in and watch the camera feeds!
